Latest News : New Board of Directors, Minutes of General Meeting and accounts for the year 2016 is under the menu item "Association".

Lochers Girls are a girl's club in Hornbæk Golf Club. We play every Thursday during the season from April 1 to September 30, except holidays.
The club is for all women who are members of Hornbæk Golf Club regardless of age and hcp, you just need to have access to the high court.
In addition, we have a few more matches and a weekend. Furthermore, there dinner 5-6 times during the season.

There booked time via GolfBox or through formiddagsmix (read more under the menu item match calendar / formiddagsmix) and we have a priority between the hours. 9:00 and 11:00, and again between 15:00 and 15:50.

The participation fee is Kr. 350, - for the whole season 2016. The amount paid into the bank account Lochers Women: Reg 0466 account no. 5367 299 357. (remember the name and DGU membership number).
A guest who is not a member of Lochers girls are welcome to attend the weekly match fee by the match fee of 40 kr. (Money put in Lochers Women mailbox). The guest participating in the prize line.

To access additional information regarding Lochers Girls and all events you must use Login via the "News and Golfbox" and then in the left menu "Member Login" Under the menu items "Match Calendar" and "Other events" you can read more about all offers and practical information.

Do you have questions Lochers Girls or comments / corrections to our site, just write to me Ina@schiermacher.net. And write topic "Lochers girls"

    • pink Cup

      Pink Cup is a nationwide tournament, with proceeds going to breast cancer women - through Cancer Society.

      Hornbæk Golf Club is organizing this year our tournament Thursday. August 11, 2016

      Invitation and further information will be sent out in the mail in early May to all women in Hornbæk Golf Club.

      But from the end of April we will start our collection by:

      Pink Bod selling div. items - including purchased from the Danish Cancer Society, and little else purchased in Thailand. The profit goes to our collection.

      Sale of tickets for tournaments, dinners, etc. - Gains will be on display, drawn and distributed on the day.

      Sandpit Tax - We urge all count "Sandpit Visit" on all rounds and then put 1 kr. Per. visit to the Pink Pig in the bar.

      Parking fee - 10 kr. To Pentecost The match on May 16

      These are interim measures, there will be regular information later.

      All this is based on voluntary work. We get really great support of Hornbæk by's business, Cafe'InGOLF, shop, Secretariat, Green keepers, as well as some of Klubben's members and sponsors.

      Last year's collection resulted in 53,494.75.

      If some of you can help other sponsors are very welcome to contact the Pink Cup Committee.
