Klubben

Hornbæk Golfklub og dets baneanlæg ejes 100% af medlemmerne, og du er som medlem således medejer af klubben, dog uden at hæfte for klubbens gæld. Dit ansvar er begrænset til betaling af indskud og kontingent.

Klubben blev stiftet 18. februar 1989 af en række initiativrige borgere i Hornbæk, som mente, at etableringen af en golfklub og golfbane i Hornbæk, ville være endnu et aktiv for vort lokalområde. Hornbæk Golfklub er fuldgyldigt medlem af Dansk Golf Union som medlemsklub nr. 67.

Endvidere er Hornbæk Golfklub medlem af paraplyorganisationen Hornbæk Idrætsforening, hvor vi i 1999 er blevet den største af de mange underafdelinger.

9 Huller

Medlemmerne har siden stiftelsen spillet på Passebækgaard - Gilleleje Golfklubs gamle bane, men kunne 4. juli 1998 indvie de første 9 huller af banen i Bøtterup.

Jordarealerne blev erhvervet i 1997, og anlægsarbejdet på de første 9 huller var tilendebragt i september 1997.

Efter at have oplevet en særdeles positiv udvikling i medlemstallet gennem 1998, besluttede bestyrelsen i foråret 1999 at anlægge de sidste 9 huller over sommeren 1999. Indvielse af de i alt 18 huller samt det nye klubhus fandt sted ved et storstilet arrangement den 15. juli 2000.

Klubben overtog i 2013 gården Lærkeager, hvor der nu er anlagt en super par-3 bane, der er åben hele året. Alle opfordres til at gå et par runder på den, teste det korte spil og putte på de fantastiske greens. På Lærkeager findes der også mulighed for indendørs vintertræning i hallen.

Siden 2001 har klubben investeret i en fortsat udbygning og forbedring af vores faciliteter, herunder baderum, sekretariat, edb-system, 1. sal på klubhuset, driving range, øveområde til indspil samt en række mindre forbedringer af banen. Dette arbejde vil fortsætte i takt med at behovene opstår, og økonomien tillader det.

Klubben har p.t. plads til flere medlemmer – både helt nye golfspillere og spillere, der har været medlem andre steder. For nye golfspillere tilrettelægges strukturerede forløb, der sikrer et fundament både praktisk og teoretisk inden man tager stilling til, om golf skal være en del af livet fremover.

Kontakt Sekretariatet for flere oplysninger.

    Generalforsamlinger

    Om afholdelse af Generalforsamling i Hornbæk Golfklub er følgende nedfældet i klubbens vedtægter:


    § 4. Generalforsamling Klubbens øverste myndighed er generalforsamlingen.

    Ordinær generalforsamling afholdes i Hornbæk hvert år inden udgangen af marts måned. Indkaldelse skal ske skriftligt med mindst 14 dages varsel med angivelse af dagsorden og vedlagt sammendrag af det reviderede regnskab. Herudover skal det detaljerede reviderede regnskab være fremlagt i klubbens sekretariat.

    Generalforsamlingen ledes af en af generalforsamlingen uden for bestyrelsen valgt dirigent.

    Stemmeberettigede er kun aktive og passive medlemmer, som i følge dansk lov er personlig myndige, og som ikke er i restance til klubben, med hver 1 stemme. Der kan ikke stemmes med fuldmagt.

    Beslutninger træffes ved almindeligt stemmeflertal, hvor disse vedtægter ikke foreskriver andet.

    På 50% af de fremmødte, stemmeberettigede medlemmers forlangende, skal der afholdes skriftlig afstemning.

    Ved valg af bestyrelsesmedlemmer og revisor skal der - i tilfælde af kampvalg - anvendes skriftlig afstemning og følgende afstemningsmetode: Der stemmes om samtlige ledige poster på en gang. Hvert stemmeberettiget medlem skal stemme på det antal kandidater, der skal vælges og kun med 1 stemme på hver kandidat. Ved stemmelighed skal omvalg straks afholdes efter samme regler mellem kandidaterne med lige stemmetal, såfremt der stadig er ubesatte poster.

    Ekstraordinær generalforsamling afholdes, når bestyrelsen bestemmer det, eller når mindst ¼ af de stemmeberettigede medlemmer fremsætter skriftlig begæring til bestyrelsen herom med angivelse af de forslag, der ønskes behandlet.

    Den ekstraordinære generalforsamling skal afholdes inden 4 uger efter begæringens modtagelse, og indkaldelse skal ske skriftligt med mindst 8 dages varsel med angivelse af dagsorden.

    Over forhandlingerne på generalforsamlingen føres en protokol, som underskrives af dirigenten.

    § 5. Dagsorden
    Dagsorden for den ordinære generalforsamling er følgende:
    1. Valg af dirigent
    2. Bestyrelsens beretning om klubbens virksomhed i det forløbne år
    3. Fremlæggelse af det reviderede regnskab til godkendelse
    4. Fremlæggelse af budget og godkendelse af medlemskontingenter og indskud
    5. Forslag fra bestyrelsen
    6. Forslag fra medlemmerne
    7. Valg af bestyrelsesmedlemmer
    8. Valg af revisor og revisorsuppleant
    9. Eventuelt

    Forslag fra medlemmerne skal, for at kunne behandles på en ordinær generalforsamling, være indgivet skriftligt til bestyrelsen senest den 1. februar. Forslag fra medlemmerne skal omtales i indkaldelsen til generalforsamlingen.

  • Vision 2020

    Hornbæk Golfklubs bestyrelse har besluttet, at der skal udarbejdes en samlet strategi for klubbens udvikling indtil 2020.

    Bestyrelsen vil indbyde et bredt udsnit af medlemmer, ansatte og vore forpagtere til at medvirke i arbejdet. Hensigten er, at vi skal bruge de mange kompetencer, som findes i klubben. Der vil endvidere blive afholdt medlemsmøder for alle klubbens medlemmer.

    Som indledning til strategiarbejdet vil der blive udarbejdet en kort beskrivelse af følgende:
    Hvilken type klub er Hornbæk Golfklub?
    Klubbens mission - hvorfor klubben er til
    Klubbens vision - vores drømme om klubbens udvikling
    De fælles værdier, som klubben er baseret på (høj etik og moral etc.)
    Dilemmaer – forskellige ønsker, der strider imod hinanden - kan hindre klubbens udvikling i den ønskede retning

    Bestyrelsen har herudover udvalgt 5 områder, der skal fokuseres på for at opnå ” VISION 2020.

    Du kan læse mere under "Fremtidens Hornbæk Golfklub" og "Strategiarbejdet"

    • Fremtidens Hornbæk Golfklub

      Hornbæk Golf Club's board has decided that there should be a single strategy for the club's development until 2020.

      The Board will invite a wide range of members, employees and our tenants to participate in the work. The intention is that we should use the many skills that are at the club. There will also be organized member meetings for all club members.

      As an introduction to the strategy work will be drawn up a short description of the following:

      What kind of club is Hornbæk Golf Club?
      The club's mission - why the club is to
      The club's vision - our dream of the club's development
      The common values ​​that the club is based on (high ethics and morals, etc.)
      Dilemmas - different desires contrary to each other - may hinder the club's development in the desired direction

      The board has also selected five areas to focus on to achieve "VISION 2020. It is as follows:
      The club's facilities and financing of new desires The club's organization and interaction between wage - and voluntary staff and tenants Communication within the club and to the outside world - and strengthening the club's "brand" (how we want the club to be perceived by members and the outside world) The club's finances or the balance between the number of members and guests, and member types, income, expenses and debt The club's social life - club in the club - sports teams, width, depreciation and approach and retention of members - who are loyal and faithful member?

      For each area will be set up a group of members who will be invited to discuss the topic. In addition, there will be a group that discusses the Board's proposal for the Mission and Vision mm. Discussions should be an inspiration for further strategy work.

      It does not mean that other areas be passively go. The other areas works acceptable and existing councils will continue the good work, including the foundation of a good golf experience, namely the field.

      Many members have answered questions from the analysis tool "golfer in the center" . The results of the members' responses will also be included in the work of Vision 2020.

      Focus groups
      Under the plan, the work of focus groups conducted in October 2014. The first meeting of members on the strategy work will be held in early December. Work on Vision 2020 - "TOMORROW Hornbæk Golf Club" will be completed with a member meeting in February 2015. If the strategy work leads to the need for amendment of the previous General Assembly resolutions, it will happen at the Annual General Meeting in March 2015. The implementation of the organization will take place subsequently.

      Managing the strategy work
      Work on Vision 2020 - "TOMORROW Hornbæk Golf Club" is led by a steering committee consisting of three board members (president Jens Peter Nielsen, Vice Chairman Carsten Carstensen and board member Morten Søgaard) assisted by two process consultants (Jørgen Sandager, who is a certified public accountant and has advisory experience with strategy work, and Torben Nielsen, who graduated university economist (cand. polit., HD (r)) and has experience as a financial adviser.

      If you have questions about the club's work with Strategy 2020, feel free to write to Jørgen Sandager (jsandager44@gmail.com ) or Torben Nielsen (torben@wonsyld.dk ).

      Board, 18 August 2014

    • Strategiarbejdet - baggrund og begreber

      Hornbæk Golf Club's situation

      Hornbæk Golf Club celebrated in July 2014 its 25th anniversary. In the first 20 years saw the club progress with growing membership every year. There were long waiting lists in established clubs around Copenhagen and North Sealand and Hornbæk Golf Club had room for more members.

      In the club's first year, changing leadership and a large part of the members spent thousands of hours to design and establish the framework that we now view as a natural and obvious part of Hornbæk Golf Club. This applies for example. course, the clubhouse, the machinery, the training facilities, bagrummene, the matches, the restaurant and a coaching staff that have the skills to improve all types of golfers.

      Then came the financial crisis with the consequences we know all too well. The number of members dropped drastically. Many members chose to find a club near their residence. The decline in the number of members began just after the club had committed itself financially with the acquisition of Lark Field. The club remains tight economy means that there is not yet to take advantage of Lark Field full.

      During these years, golf clubs also started to notice that there are more older people and fewer young people in the population. For older ceases golf played by natural causes within a limited number of years and younger have many choices when choosing leisure activities for themselves and their children.

      Most golf clubs have abolished the waiting lists, and the economy is under pressure. The development has led to increased competition between the clubs include with "cheap deals" to gain new members. Right now missing most of our neighboring clubs around 100 members to achieve financial freedom to maintain a modern golf club with the amenities demanded of a modern service society.

      Hornbæk Golf Club has in recent years lost 10-15% of the members of each year. We must expect this trend to continue in the coming years. There is therefore every year recruited a large number of new members just to maintain member population.

      In 2013 saw the club for the first time since 2009, a slight increase in the number of full-time members. In addition, there are more flex members. The first half of 2014, has also shown a positive trend in membership.

      The positive development is due not least to the ingenuity loaded in marketing for new members. And so we must remember to thank the volunteers who accept the new golfers and give them a good introduction to the club, so they join.

      Work on the par 3 course progresses as planned, so the circuit can be put into service in spring 2015 and the club loan has been swapped to a lower interest rate. Apparently, the future prospects for the club brighter than 1-2 years ago. However, we must remember that every year to recruit new members to maintain balance in the club's finances. If we want to raise money to develop Lark Ager or to other new projects in the club, it is necessary that we get even more new members - much like 100 new full-time members - or that we find other forms of financing.

      Why is there a need for strategy work?
      Hornbæk Golf Club is both a sport and a business. The fixed, daily work is performed by paid employees. At the same time performs many members volunteer work that is necessary for the club to operate. The Board will also volunteering.

      Our neighbor clubs has increased membership fee to obtain a greater financial scope for improvement of facilities mm. Hornbæk Golf Club, however, argue quota at a lower level. The Board has therefore chosen to use the most energy on daily operations, recruiting new members and the establishment of par 3 pitch Lark Ager.

      When we look back, the Board of restricted resources has been used to guide the club through the everyday problems. There have been several attempts to work more long term, but it has not been completed.

      The increased competition for members and more sensitive economy has shown that management of a golf club has become a major challenge. This also applies to Hornbæk Golf Club. This is the background for the Board's decision to start work on a joint strategy for the club.

      Strategy Process
      Strategy process will form the framework of Vision 2020 - "TOMORROW Hornbæk Golf Club" and should result in a decision on the club's mission, vision, values and strategic goals. The purpose of the vision work is to create a clear picture of the potential and to further develop the club. We want Hornbæk Golf Club for members is the favorite among the member-owned clubs in Northern Zealand and attractive to visitors. The club must be known as part of Hornbæk city's fire, and a club that members are proud to be a part. This applies course, clubhouse, practice facilities, staff, sports department and not least, the atmosphere and unity.

      Strategy is to choose from and from what we dream of will and can. A good strategy is based on facts about the development and the anticipated trends. The strategy builds on existing strengths. It must be based on an innovative vision, a "dream," which would be unattainable. The vision should address all Hornbæk Golf Club's value chain and take the starting point in the club's values, culture and organization and supporting good golf experiences in an inclusive social environment and a service that is beyond what we expect. At the same time the use of the potential that is within the club's many strands.

      Hornbæk Golf Club can as a member-owned club function optimally only with the help of the club's many volunteers skills. The potential is great if the right volunteer resources can be found and activated. For this to be realized, the organization adapted to future challenges.

      Mission, vision and values
      Board began at a seminar in October 2013 to discuss the overall framework of the strategy process:

      MISSION
      "Why and for whom the club is for and what we must do to achieve maximum satisfaction among users of the club?"

      VISION
      "How is the club's idéal state?" - "A dream" in 2020, which would be difficult to meet

      CORE VALUES
      "Common understanding and expectations of management, employees and members' behavior - what the internal relations is based on (eg, inclusiveness, trust, support, morals, ethics and honesty and democracy, etc.). What we emphasize, and what limits we have? "

      Goals and action plans
      in order to make a vision a reality if the vision is translated into concrete and measurable strategic objectives in the short or long term should be pursued. Examples of goals:
      80% of the members must be satisfied with the club The club's sports teams to be among the top 3 in the rows in which they participate The restaurant is to create social life, and 80% of the customers to be satisfied The net inflow of members must be at least 20 per. years and higher than in our neighboring clubs
      The targets are divided on the club's many committees and all the important elements of the whole, which will constitute Vision 2020 - "TOMORROW Hornbæk Golf Club." Based on the strategic objectives which should be specific, measurable, ambitious, realistic, personalized and engaging, drawn up action plans and annual budgets for each of the club's organizational units. Action plans and annual budgets must then be approved by the board and be part of the club's overall activity planning and budget.

      Based on the strategic plan, including the strategic goals drawn up budgets and forecasts of revenue and expenditure until 2020. When the future is uncertain, requires alternative scenarios that are part of the decision making until 2020.

      Vision Report 2020

      The finished work is assembled in a VISION REPORT 2020 a year to be assessed and possibly adjusted by the Board at a strategy meeting. For future boards report will be:
      • A management tool for use by priority, set the direction and stay focused on the club's development.
      • A tool to clearly communicate the resulting demands on the development, roles and potential wage -and volunteers, employees, tenants / partners and members • A support tool for board and committee for the design of decentralized strategy -and action plans
      • A tool to communicate "stories" to the outside world, including sponsors and green fee guests and new members to strengthen the club's brand and thus economy.
